Snap It

Take a photo of something around you and we’ll show you something similar in our collection. Try it out on a household item or the watch on your wrist. You might be surprised by what is in our collection.

Works best with a clear photo of a single object, well-lit, against a plain background. Setup the first time takes around 30 seconds.

The photo is processed in your browser. We only send an anonymised representation of the image to our servers to find matches.
