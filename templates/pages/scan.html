Scan an object

Point your camera at an object in our museums (or a printed photo) to find the closest matches in our collection.

Works best with a clear photo of a single object, well-lit, against a plain background. Setup the first time you scan takes around 30 seconds.

The photo is processed in your browser. We only send an anonymised representation of the image to our servers to find matches.
